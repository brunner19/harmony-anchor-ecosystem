# Safety And Legal Disclaimer

Harmony Anchor is a civic technology prototype. It helps communities organize evidence, draft materials, and compare upgrade alternatives. It does not provide legal advice, architectural certification, emergency response, financial advice, or official determinations.

## Legal Review Required

All legal materials are drafts for qualified Nigerian counsel. Do not file, serve, publish, or rely on generated legal documents without review by a lawyer familiar with the facts, the court, the agencies involved, and the current law.

## Evidence Safety

Evidence collection can create retaliation risk. Teams should:

- Collect only what is necessary.
- Get consent before recording or sharing.
- Preserve originals offline.
- Separate private household records from public summaries.
- Redact children, precise home locations, phone numbers, and sensitive testimony unless disclosure is approved.
- Avoid confrontation when documenting enforcement or equipment movement.

## Architecture And Engineering Review

Upgrade plans are concept drafts. Licensed architects, engineers, planners, environmental specialists, and community representatives must validate all design, flood, sanitation, electrical, structural, and fire-safety decisions before construction.

## AI Transparency

Any future LLM-backed deployment should tell users when AI assisted the workflow. Agent outputs should be labeled as draft, reviewed, approved, rejected, or escalated. Prompt versions and source data categories should be audit-visible.

## Emergency Use

Harmony Anchor is not an emergency service. If immediate danger, active violence, urgent medical need, or an ongoing demolition is present, users should contact trusted local emergency, legal, community, or protection channels available in their area.

## Source Verification

The MVP includes reference links for the UN eviction guidelines, African Charter, Nigerian Land Use Act, and Lagos planning law. These links must be re-verified before production or court use because law, official websites, and procedures can change.
